All About Olympics

The Olympic games is the largest multi-sport event in the world held every four years. It has eyes over it from across the globe. Athletes which are best
among their countries participate to compete against each other and show their capabilities. It receives a great deal of attention from all age groups.
The ultimate goals are to cultivate human beings, through sport, and contribute to world peace. Summer Games and Winter Games are held separately.
